The Scott family is linked to Jarvis family through Alice May Scott who married Eric Roy Jarvis of Adelaide, South Australia. Alice is grandmother to the author and many memories are of Uncles and Aunts from the Scott family.

The Desmond family


Alice's brother, Arnold, married Violet Moon and they had three daughters, Norma, Joyce and Rhonda. Norma Scott married Ray Desmond leading to the Desmond family, as shown in the Scott family tree. Three image albums have been created from photos provided by Greg Desmond, grandson of Arnold Scott.
